Brakes

 

Brake issues can be caused by a variety of factors such as loss of brake fluid, worn out brake pads or shoes, bad brake cylinders, and a bad brake booster. Other causes include oil or grease on brakes which interferes with friction, overheating of brakes which causes hard spots in the metal of brake rotors or drums, and air being introduced into the braking system. Some symptoms that would cause concern for brake issues include a soft brake pedal, a grinding noise when braking, a vibration when braking, and a burning smell when driving.

Debrick Mobile can help you with your brake issues by providing a mobile mechanic service that comes to you. They can change your brake pads and rotors on site and provide a full brake inspection to ensure your vehicle is safe to drive.

Transmission Service

 

Transmission service is an essential part of maintaining your vehicle’s health and longevity. The transmission fluid and filter should be changed periodically to ensure proper functioning of your vehicle. The recommended service interval may vary depending on your vehicle’s model, age, and usage, but it is usually every 30,000-60,000 miles or every two years. You can also check the transmission fluid dipstick for signs of dark or burnt-smelling fluid, which indicate that the fluid and filter need to be changed. Changing the transmission fluid and filter may be a messy or difficult task, as some transmission pans don’t have drain plugs and the entire pan must be removed.

At Debrick Mobile, we offer transmission services that include changing the transmission fluid and filter. Our experienced technicians will remove the old fluid from your vehicle’s transmission pan and replace it with new fluid that meets your vehicle’s manufacturer specifications. We will also replace your vehicle’s transmission filter to ensure proper functioning of your vehicle. We use specialized equipment to flush and refill your vehicle’s transmission with new fluid. This equipment ensures that all of the old fluid is removed from your vehicle’s transmission before new fluid is added. Our technicians will also inspect your vehicle’s transmission pan for any signs of damage or wear and complete repairs if necessary.
